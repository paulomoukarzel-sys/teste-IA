The Firm

Traditional advocacy of excellence

Family tradition in providing legal services of excellence to Santa Catarina's society

The firm Gastão da Rosa & Moukarzel Advogados Associados was founded in 2022 by Bruno Gastão da Rosa and Paulo Ekke Moukarzel Jr., attorneys with extensive experience in criminal, civil, corporate, and administrative law, encompassing administrative improbity, administrative disciplinary proceedings, as well as contract, real estate, possessory, family, and succession law.

Based in Florianópolis, the firm was born from the convergence of tradition and a focus on providing highly qualified legal services to its clients. Bruno carries the legacy of a distinguished lineage in criminal law in Santa Catarina — his father, Dr. Cláudio Gastão da Rosa, built one of the most brilliant and respected careers in criminal law in the state.

We practice in the administrative sphere and at all levels of the Judiciary, accompanying our clients throughout the entire legal relationship, including the phase preceding litigation and investigation, through the superior courts, always with ethics, absolute confidentiality, and personalized service. Each case receives exclusive attention and an individualized strategy, accompanying our clients on an individual and personalized basis, from the investigative phase to the superior courts — always committed to adopting the best management and ethical practices in the conduct of legal cases, while ensuring confidentiality for our clients and prioritizing high-quality personalized service.

Gastão da Rosa & Moukarzel Advogados Associados believes that this adopted approach is fundamental to building lasting relationships of trust and achieving positive results for our clients.

Our Values

Principles That Guide Our Practice

Ethics & Confidentiality

Absolute commitment to professional confidentiality and ethics in our relationship with clients and the justice system.

Technical Excellence

Continuous qualification and study, combined with the partners' constant updating in their areas of legal practice, constitute essential pillars of our firm, deliver legal assistance of excellence in the defense of our clients' rights and interests, in their fullest extent.

Personalized Service

Every case is unique. We dedicate individual attention and legal advisory, provided entirely by at least one of the firm's founding partners, as a way to deliver all the technical expertise of our firm and offer a tailored strategy, considering all the particularities of each client and the respective legal situation of their case.

Availability

Constant presence at every stage where legal advisory is necessary, with the possibility of acting from the moment preceding the formation of the legal relationship, including extrajudicial negotiations and the defense of clients' interests during the judicial process itself.
