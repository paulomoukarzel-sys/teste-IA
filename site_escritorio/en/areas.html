Practice Areas

We practice in diverse areas of Law, with specialization and consolidated experience in criminal, civil, corporate, and administrative matters. Learn about our specialties.

Criminal Law

Criminal defense at all court levels, including Jury Trials for intentional crimes against life, money laundering, and crimes against the economic and financial order. Practice from the phase preceding the investigation through superior courts.

Civil Law

Advisory and litigation in civil matters, including civil liability, contracts, obligations, and indemnification claims. Comprehensive support from the preventive and pre-litigation phase through judgment enforcement.

Corporate Law

Preventive and contentious corporate legal consulting and advisory, including corporate and contract law. Practice from the establishment of the legal relationship through conflict resolution.

Administrative Improbity & Disciplinary Proceedings

Defense in administrative improbity actions and administrative disciplinary proceedings. Strategic practice from the phase preceding the initiation of proceedings through appeals to superior courts.

Real Estate & Property Law

Comprehensive advisory on real estate and property matters, including adverse possession, land regularization, purchase and sale agreements, leasing, and real estate development. Support from the pre-contractual phase through dispute resolution.

Family & Succession Law

Practice in divorce, dissolution of stable unions, custody, alimony, probate, estate division, and wills. Advisory from the mediation and extrajudicial negotiation phase through the conclusion of proceedings.
