The Firm

The Firm

The firm Gastão da Rosa & Moukarzel Advogados Associados was born from the union of two professionals deeply dedicated to criminal, civil, administrative and corporate law, also practicing in the areas of administrative improbity and administrative disciplinary proceedings, contract law, real estate, possessory, family and succession law, combining family tradition and specialized technical knowledge in the firm's practice areas.

The service provided by the firm is specialized and conducted directly by the founding partners, ensuring that each client receives personalized legal advisory, with a strategy built around the specificities of their case and individualized professional follow-up throughout every stage of the legal relationship — from the first contact to the conclusion of the matter.

Based in Florianópolis/SC, we practice in all levels of the Judiciary, accompanying our clients individually and in a personalized manner, as well as preventively, from the investigation phase through superior courts — always meeting the highest standards of ethics, confidentiality, and prioritizing individualized quality service.

The firm Gastão da Rosa & Moukarzel Advogados Associados has, among its partners, a member of a family with a solid tradition in criminal law in Santa Catarina, and offers decades of accumulated legal experience to its clients, bringing a deep understanding of both the defense perspective and an in-depth analysis of the strategy adopted by the opposing party and the prosecution.

Partner Bruno Gastão da Rosa is an attorney dedicated to criminal defense, with specialized technical training in Criminal Law and Criminal Procedure and practical experience that gives him a singular command of both the prosecution and defense perspectives — a quality that translates into differentiated and effective procedural strategy in the handling of each case.

Partner Paulo Ekke Moukarzel Jr. brings to the firm a broad legal background and a professional trajectory marked by practice across different areas of law — from the Public Prosecution Service to civil, criminal and corporate litigation. Graduated in Law since 2012, with experience at leading institutions including the Superior School of the Judiciary of Santa Catarina and the Public Prosecution Service of Santa Catarina. The partner combines technical rigor and strategic vision with individualized service throughout every stage of the matter, with constant presence and unrestricted commitment to the defense of clients' rights and interests.

Specialties

Practice Areas

Criminal Law

Criminal defense at all court levels, including Jury Trials for intentional crimes against life, money laundering, and crimes against the economic and financial order. Practice from the phase preceding the investigation through superior courts.

Civil Law

Advisory and litigation in civil matters, including civil liability, contracts, obligations, and indemnification claims. Comprehensive support from the preventive and pre-litigation phase through judgment enforcement.

Corporate Law

Preventive and contentious corporate legal consulting and advisory, including corporate and contract law. Practice from the establishment of the legal relationship through conflict resolution.

Administrative Improbity & Disciplinary Proceedings

Defense in administrative improbity actions and administrative disciplinary proceedings. Strategic practice from the phase preceding the initiation of proceedings through appeals to superior courts.

Real Estate & Property Law

Comprehensive advisory on real estate and property matters, including adverse possession, land regularization, purchase and sale agreements, leasing, and real estate development. Support from the pre-contractual phase through dispute resolution.

Family & Succession Law

Practice in divorce, dissolution of stable unions, custody, alimony, probate, estate division, and wills. Advisory from the mediation and extrajudicial negotiation phase through the conclusion of proceedings.
